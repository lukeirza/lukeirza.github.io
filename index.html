A Weighted Flow Accumulation Model of Sheep and Cattle Contribution to Fecal Contamination in the Bay of Poverty and Gisborne Region

This is my final GIS Capstone Project for UCSD Extension. Gisborne, New Zealand is a beautiful city located on the east coast of the North Island. The country is perhaps best known for its wine, sheep, and green mussels. Fecal coliform contamination was previously shown to be a huge hurdle for the establishment of an aquaculture industry in Gisborne. In this project, I examine the issue of E. coli in runoff from grazing land using ArcGIS and ESRI Model Builder.
